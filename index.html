About Us


I am a tour operator having experience of more than 10 years of organizing tours in Ladakh. I drive my Mahindra Xylo (7-seater) and can connect with more taxi drivers if required for larger groups.
During the off-season,I am also engaged by army officials when they travel from Leh to border areas.

Unparalleled know-how of Ladakh:

  • Umling La Pass in Ladakh, at an altitude of 19024 Feet, and the Guinness World recorder holder for the world’s highest pass.
  • Sham Valley which includes Gurudwara Pathar Sahib, Magnetic Hill, and Sangam (confluence of Zanskar and Indus).
  • Nubra Valley which includes Diskit Monastery.
  • Turtuk (part of Balochistan), and Thang (border village from where one can see the Indo- Pak border).
  • Siachen Base Camp (the highest battlefield in the world).
  • High-altitude lakes like Pangong Tso and Tso - Moriri.
  • Hanle (star gazers paradise)
  • High-altitude mountain passes like Khardungla, Changla, and Tangang La.
  • Chumathang Wildlife Sanctuary region around Hanle.

Inclusions:

  • Comfortable Vehicle (Xylo) for sightseeing on all days as per the itinerary
  • Airport pick up & drop as per your flight timings
  • Fuel for the whole trip as per the itinerary
  • Ladakh Union fee
  • Oxygen cylinder and Basic First-Aid during the tour
  • Driver expenses/ toll tax/ Fuel/ Parking charges
Note - Permits, wildlife guides,hotel/homestay accommodation and trekking expeditions etc. can be arranged.
